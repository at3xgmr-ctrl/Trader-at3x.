How to read: The cyan line is BTC price. Green line = 20-period Moving Average (short trend). Purple line = 50-period MA (long trend). When price crosses above the MA → potential BUY signal. When price falls below → potential SELL signal.
RSI — Relative Strength Index
RSI Guide: Above 70 = Overbought (consider selling). Below 30 = Oversold (consider buying). 40–60 = Neutral zone.

Volume Guide: Green bars = buying pressure. Red bars = selling pressure. High volume confirms price movements — low volume signals weak moves.
